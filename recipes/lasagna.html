Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta,possibly one of the oldest types,made of very widde,flat sheets.

Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragu,bechamel sauce,vegetables etc

Ingridients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Italian sausage
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Egg
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook noodles,drain.Meanwhile,cook sausage,beef and onion until meat is nolonger pink,breaking up meat into crumbles.Add garlic,cook 1 minute.Drain
  2. Stir in tomatoes,water,sugar,salt and pepper,bring to aboil,reduce heat,simmer uncovered,30 minues,stirring occassionally.
  3. Mix egg,ricotta cheese and salt.
  4. Preheat,spread 2 cups of meat sauce into an ungreased baking dish.Layer with noodles and a portion of the ricotta mixture.Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and parmesan cheese.Repeat layers twice.Top with te remaining meat sauce and cheeses(dish will be full).
  5. Bake covered for 25 minutes.Bake uncovered for 25 minutes longer or until bubbly.Let stand for 15 minutes before serving.
